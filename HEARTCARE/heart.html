Heath Care

Angioplasty & Stent Placement

A minimally invasive procedure to open blocked heart arteries. Stents (small mesh tubes) are placed to keep arteries open and improve blood flow.

Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery (CABG)

Also known as bypass surgery, it redirects blood around blocked arteries. Improves blood flow to the heart and prevents heart attacks.

Heart Valve Repair & Replacement

Treats damaged heart valves caused by aging, infections, or congenital defects. Valve repair or artificial valve replacement restores heart function.

Pacemaker & ICD Implantation

Pacemaker: Regulates abnormal heartbeats (bradycardia or arrhythmia). Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD): Prevents life-threatening heart rhythm disorders.

Heart Failure Management

Medications, lifestyle changes, and advanced therapies for heart failure. Includes cardiac rehabilitation programs to improve heart function.
