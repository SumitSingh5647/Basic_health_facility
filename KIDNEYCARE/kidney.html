Kidney Care

Dialysis (Hemodialysis & Peritoneal Dialysis)

Removes waste, extra fluid, and toxins from the blood when kidneys fail. Hemodialysis: Uses a machine to filter blood outside the body

Kidney Transplant

Replacement of a damaged kidney with a healthy donor kidney. Includes pre-transplant evaluation, surgery, and post-transplant care.

Kidney Stone Treatment

Lithotripsy: Shock wave therapy to break kidney stones. Ureteroscopy & Laser Therapy: Minimally invasive procedures to remove stones.

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Management

Slows disease progression with medication, lifestyle changes, and diet plans. Manages risk factors like diabetes and high blood pressure.

Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) Treatment

Emergency treatment for sudden kidney failure due to infections, dehydration, or toxins. Includes temporary dialysis or medication to restore kidney function.
